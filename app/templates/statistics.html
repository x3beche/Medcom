What is done in the communities?

Students can prepare for competitions and organize events by cooperating with each other. You can click on the boxes at main page to get more specific information about each community.

What does MEDCOM do?

MEDCOM enables Istanbul Medipol University engineering communities to be followed from a single point and to distribute important announcements concerning the communities. Apart from that, it allows teams to collaborate by keeping records of their announcements, statistics and activities.
